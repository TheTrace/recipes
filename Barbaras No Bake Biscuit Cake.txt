Melt together in a pan (pot):
    4 oz butter (113g)
    3 oz syurp (85g)
    1/2 oz cocoa butter (14g)
    8 oz crushed digestives (227g)
    [2 oz raisins (57g)]
When thoroughly mixed press into well greased tin
Let this cool in fridge whilst melting a large bar of white chocolate
Then spread over and decorate with swirls of dark chocolate (3 or 4 squares) and pecan nuts
Mark the slices when just beginning to set
Loose bottom tin and push out ??
